Drug Interaction Risk Explorer

A story about two molecules · DrugBank relation type + TwoSides side effects · one shared GNN encoder
📄 IEEE paper ⚙ GitHub 🤗 HF Space ⚠ research use only

Give the model two drugs as SMILES strings and it tells you the full story: (1) what the molecules look like, (2) what kind of interaction the literature would label, (3) which side effects the post-marketing surveillance signal links to this combo, and (4) how the model arrived there.
